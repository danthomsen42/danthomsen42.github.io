imdb.com | International Movie Database


Target Audience

1. I would say that the target Audience of the International Movie Database
would be anyone, though more especially those who are old enough to care
about looking into said movies, so probably around 14 and up, and there
really isn't any other specific demographic it targets

2. The website's purpose is well stated in its name. It is a database of
most any movie. It has a good amount of space and only shows a limited
amount of items on it at a time so as not to overhelm the user.

3. The website's organization is very well designed. It has trailers for
new releases and upcoming films right at the front. Underneath those
the links to the showtimes and ticket info are right there. The search
bar is right at the top and is easily seen and accessable.

4. The nicest part of the website was that it was so user friendly. Not
only was the search engine very easy to use, the organization was very
nice and finding information on certain movies was extremely easy such
as parental reviews and guides.

5. I would say that the website is very well optimized for those using a
mobile device. Individuals I believe would enjoy using its service.
